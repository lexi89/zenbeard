Lets work together

If you want to join this little movement of ours, I'd love to talk to you. Here's what I suggest you do:

First, read the About Zenbeard page to understand the mission and the topics we cover.

Then, take a look around the categories at the top and read a few posts to get a feel for what people like to read here.

And finally, if you have something constructive to use the form below to send me a direct message.

Also, if you don't know what you'd like to write about, send a message anyway and we'll bounce some ideas.

I reply to every message but please wait a bit — I might not reply right away.

Looking forward to it!

Lex
