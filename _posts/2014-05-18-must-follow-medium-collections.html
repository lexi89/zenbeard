Medium is the beautiful baby of Twitter founders, Ev Williams and Biz Stone. There are many ways to describe it, but for me - it is simply the best platform for storytelling on the internet.

In an age where anyone can publish things that look like crap and read like crap, Medium separates the wheat from the chaff.

Medium lets anybody submit a story, without having to worry about the formatting - it is already perfect and will do your work justice.

People can curate collections of stories around a theme, which you "follow", and your homepage is a stream of stuff that YOU like to read.
