There are two things that I have always found fascinating about smoothies.

 

1) It is IMPOSSIBLE to make a smoothie that doesn't taste good (please disprove me on this)

 

2) Nobody knows why fruit and veg tastes incredible when blended together

 

Making a smoothie is so easy even a toddler could do it and it makes getting your 5-a-day delicious and fast. And we love a good shortcut, don't we, gents?

 

Start your day off like a champ with one of these badmen:

 

 

"The Big 5"

Purpose: ALL of your 5 a day in a cup. SIMPLE AS THAT.

 

Ingredients:

- an orange (squeeze)

- an apple (chopped)

- a carrot

- a stick of celery

- a beetroot

 

 

Banana Ginger

Purpose: if you had a questionable kebab that has left you a bit gassy, this little hero will sort you out.

 

- A banana

- 1/4 cup vanilla yoghurt

- A tablespoon of honey

- A big pinch of freshly grated ginger

 

 

"El Classico"

Purpose: Being the most powerful start to the day a man can get.

 

- A cup of non-fat yoghurt

- a banana

- 1/2 cup of orange juice

- A handful of frozen (or fresh) strawberries

 

 

Strawberry-Kiwi madness

Purpose: To give you a Vit-C overdose. Keep away from children and adolescent boys. In case you didn't know, kiwis are the best fruit ever.

 

- 1/2 cup of cold apple juice

- A banana

- A kiwi (peeled and sliced)

- Handful of strawberries

- A teaspoon of honey

 

 

Berry Boost

Purpose: a roundhouse kick to the liver of goodness and energy

 

- A cup of chopped strawberries

- A cup of blueberries

- 1/2 cup of raspberries

- A tablespoon of honey

- A teaspoon of lemon juice

- 1/2 cup of ice cubes

 

Go forth and make today the best one yet.
