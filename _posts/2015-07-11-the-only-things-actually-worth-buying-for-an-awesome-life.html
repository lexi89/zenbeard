--- title: The Only Things Actually WORTH Buying For An Awesome Life date: 2015-07-11 12:39:41.000000000 +02:00 categories: life-design ---

You want to "collect memories, not things"?

Thrive with just the bare essentials?

Never spend a single calorie on any fashion-related decision?

That's music to my ears, brother. Take a seat...

Living minimally is tough because most of the stuff out there isn't designed to last.

It's usually more profitable to design things that break down after a year so suckers like us have to get a new one.

But luckily, there is still a core of people who believe in quality and building things that are genuine investments.

It won't happen overnight, but what you're looking to do is collect a tiny amount of things that are: long-lasting, low-maintenance, and (preferably) multi-functional.

So, I've scoured through hundreds of brands, survival experts, and nomads to pick out gems that fit the bill.

Here's the list.

I'll keep updating this as new things come up so be sure to bookmark this page and keep checking in from time to time.

(Last updated: June 2015)

Last thought:

If you, like me, give zero f**ks about things because you'd rather be out there making magic happen, then you're going to want to get used to investing in something that will outlive you and your children rather than buying soft, disposable crap.

Start small and build up a core over time. If you can't buy it, try to make it yourself. Sometimes, a DIY alternative lasts longer than factory made.

Either way, make sure you keep your belongings to a minimum and start thinking about what mountain to climb or ocean to cross.
